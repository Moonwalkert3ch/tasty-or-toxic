About Us

The creators of this project are fascinated by the compelling and useful world of edible and poisonous plants that one might come across in the outdoors. For those who enjoy hiking as a hobby, please utilize our site to cross reference plants for their edibility.

DISCLAIMER: The information presented here is brought to you by a third-party API.
